## Quick Facts About Your Privacy

✅ **No account required** — MinteCalc does not create or store user accounts on our servers  
✅ **Your data stays with you** — All calculations, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device  
✅ **Optional cloud backup** — Pro users can optionally backup to their own Google Drive  
✅ **You're in control** — Delete your data anytime directly from the app or your device settings

## About MinteCalc

MinteCalc is a feature-rich calculator app available on iOS and Android, offering:
- **Standard & Scientific Calculator** — Everyday and advanced calculations
- **Note Calculator** — Add items with name, price, and quantity for easy totals
- **Currency Converter** — Convert between world currencies with hourly updated rates
- **Unit Converter** — Convert measurements across various unit types
- And more!
